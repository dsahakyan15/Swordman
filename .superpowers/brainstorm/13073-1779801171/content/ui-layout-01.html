UI Rules

  • Monospace everywhere, uppercase labels, no rounded corners.
  • Instant hover state changes, no soft gradients in controls.
  • Every image and tiled background uses [image-rendering:pixelated].

Interaction Rules

  • Header keeps burger menu on mobile and direct nav on desktop.
  • Approve, bid, and settle states show a pixel loader and then a small toast.
  • Expired open auctions stay visible and switch primary action to Settle.
